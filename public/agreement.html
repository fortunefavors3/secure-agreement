Surplus Funds Recovery Service Agreement

Please review this agreement carefully before signing.

1. Parties

This Agreement is entered into between CheqMart Recovery Services (“Service Provider”) and the undersigned individual (“Client”).

2. Services Provided

The Service Provider agrees to research, prepare, and submit claims to recover surplus or excess funds held by a county or governmental authority on behalf of the Client.

3. Fee & Compensation

Client agrees to pay the Service Provider a contingency fee of 30% of all recovered surplus funds.

Direct Payment Stipulation:
Client authorizes the county or holding authority to issue payment directly to the Service Provider, who will then remit the Client’s remaining balance after deducting the agreed fee.

4. No Recovery, No Fee

If no surplus funds are successfully recovered, the Client owes nothing.

5. Authorization

Client authorizes the Service Provider to act as their representative for purposes of surplus funds recovery, including submitting documentation and communicating with relevant authorities.

6. Electronic Signature & Consent

Client agrees that this agreement may be executed electronically. The electronic signature below shall be considered legally binding.
